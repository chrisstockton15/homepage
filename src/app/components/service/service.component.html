Service

As eager as I am to persue my career in computer science, I feel it is equally as important to give back to the community that I gain so much from. Here are a few of the charities and organizations that I participate in.

Big Event

Each year in the spring, the members of my fraternity join in being apart of the Big Event. Big Event is a community service event hosted by Texas A&M every spring, where different groups and organizations travel to different homes across College Station and provide service in any way we can. You can check out big event too at https://bigevent.tamu.edu/

LTlov

LTlov is a local charity organization that benefits underprivileged kids in the Lake Travis area. Every summer I help sort school supplies with LTlov's school supply drive to help less fortunate kids make sure they have all the supplies the need to go to school in the fall. Check them out at https://ltlov.org

Garrett's House

Every year, the fraternity that I'm apart of, Kappa Alpha Order, hosts a fundraising event in honor of our fallen brother, Garrett Luce. All proceeds go to Garrett's House, which is an orphanage in Zambia. You can learn more about this cause here

Muscular Distrophy Association

Our fraternity also helps raise money every year with a fundraising event during parents weekend at Texas A&M with all proceeds going to the Muscular Dystrophy Association (MDA). MDA helps fund the efforts to find a cure for Muscular Dystrophy, get care to more people, and send affected kids to summer camps. In the last four decades ,our organization has helped raise over $2.7 million for the MDA. To donate to or find out more about the MDA, go to https://www.mda.org
