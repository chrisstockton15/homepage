About Me

I was born in Dublin, Ireland and I grew up in Austin, Texas. My family moved here on a one-year contract when I was a kid, and we loved Texas so much we never moved back. As a first generation American, I feel like I have a uniqe perspective on American culture.

I graduated Lake Travis High School in 2017. My high school offered two computer science classes and I took the first my junior year. This was when I knew I wanted to persue a career in CS. I can still remember writing my first "hello world" program today.

Outside of work, my favorite things to do are watcing sports, surfing on the lake, and skiing in the mountains. I'm a die-hard Manchester United and Dallas Cowboys fan, even though both teams love to dissapoint me.
